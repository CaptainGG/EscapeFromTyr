# Level Content Design Skill

Turn mechanics into playable spaces and encounter flow.

## Deliverables
Write or update:
- `docs/levels.md`
- level-related sections in `docs/GDD.md`

## Output
Include:
1. Level or map purpose
2. Player learning goals
3. Pacing beats
4. Encounter structure
5. Objective structure
6. Gating and progression
7. Enemy or hazard placement logic
8. Reward placement logic
9. Reuse or procgen assumptions if any
10. Data representation notes for implementation

## Principles
Teach before testing, escalate complexity gradually, avoid filler spaces, and keep the first vertical slice small and complete.
