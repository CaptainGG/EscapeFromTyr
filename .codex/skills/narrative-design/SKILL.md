# Narrative Design Skill

Design narrative elements that strengthen player motivation, world coherence, thematic identity, mission context, emotional tone, and mechanic-story alignment.

## Deliverables
Write or update:
- `docs/narrative.md`
- the narrative sections of `docs/GDD.md`

## Output
Include:
1. High concept
2. Player role
3. World and setting
4. Tone and emotional texture
5. Core conflict
6. Key characters or archetypes
7. Factions or forces
8. Story delivery method
9. Narrative constraints for production scope
10. How narrative supports the gameplay loop

## Rules
Keep scope indie-sized, avoid lore bloat, favor evocative clarity over encyclopedic detail, build narrative around repeated play actions, and keep dialogue concise and implementation-friendly.
